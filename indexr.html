THOUGHTFULLY

An architectural design that lets in more sunlight, and superior wind channeling that enables higher air re-circulations per hour. The residents will not have to switch on the lights until sundown and an open window will almost always make way for a gentle breeze.
It has been designed with the understanding that it's always better to build around nature rather than over it. A detailed environmental audit accounted for the entire native species of trees, wildlife, topographical features, and just about everything inherited from Mother Nature.
